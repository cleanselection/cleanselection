# Code of Conduct

Be considerate, specific, and constructive. Discuss the work rather than attacking the person. Harassment, threats, discrimination, deliberate humiliation, and publication of another person's private information are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's security-advisory contact when public discussion would expose someone to further harm.
